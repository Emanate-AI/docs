# Product Roadmap

A look at what's coming to Emanate. This roadmap is subject to change based on customer feedback and priorities.

## Q1 2024

### In Progress

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="SMS Campaigns" icon="message">
    Automated SMS follow-up sequences integrated with voice campaigns. Send text messages before/after calls for higher engagement.
    
    **Status:** In Development
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Advanced Analytics" icon="chart-mixed">
    Enhanced analytics dashboard with custom report builder, scheduled exports, and AI-generated insights.
    
    **Status:** In Development
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Webhook Builder" icon="webhook">
    Visual webhook configuration with testing tools and payload previews.
    
    **Status:** Planning
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Planned

- **Email Sequences**: Automated email follow-up after conversations
- **A/B Testing**: Test agent variations to optimize performance
- **Custom Dashboards**: Build your own analytics views

## Q2 2024

### Planned Features

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="AI Call Summaries" icon="sparkles">
    Automatic call summaries generated by AI, pushed to CRM notes and email.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Multi-Language Support" icon="globe">
    Support for Spanish, French, German, and Portuguese voice agents.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Salesforce Native Integration" icon="cloud">
    Direct Salesforce integration without Merge, with deeper field mapping.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Mobile App" icon="mobile">
    iOS and Android app for monitoring campaigns and leads on the go.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Q3 2024

### On the Horizon

- **HIPAA Compliance**: Healthcare-ready with BAA support
- **WhatsApp Integration**: WhatsApp Business API for chat
- **Predictive Lead Scoring**: ML-based lead scoring
- **Voice Cloning**: Custom voice creation from samples

## Q4 2024

### Future Vision

- **API v2**: Enhanced API with GraphQL support
- **Marketplace**: Third-party integrations and templates
- **Enterprise SSO**: SAML/OIDC authentication
- **Multi-Region**: EU and APAC data centers
