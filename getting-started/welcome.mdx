## Platform Features

### Voice Agents

AI agents that handle phone calls with human-like conversation:

- **Inbound Calls**: Answer customer inquiries, qualify leads, schedule meetings
- **Outbound Campaigns**: Reach prospects at scale with personalized conversations
- **Call Recording**: Full transcripts and recordings for every conversation
- **Phone Numbers**: Provision local or toll-free numbers instantly

### Chat Agents

Embeddable chat widgets for your website:

- **Custom Branding**: Match your website's look and feel
- **Knowledge Base**: Answer questions from your documentation
- **Lead Capture**: Collect contact information naturally in conversation
- **Handoff**: Transfer to human agents when needed

### Workflow Squads

Multi-agent orchestration for complex workflows:

- **Agent Coordination**: Multiple specialized agents working together
- **Conditional Routing**: Route conversations based on intent
- **Escalation Paths**: Automatic handoff to specialists

### Lead Management

Complete lead lifecycle management:

- **Automatic Capture**: Extract contact info from conversations
- **Apollo Enrichment**: Append company and contact data
- **ICP Scoring**: AI-powered ideal customer matching
- **CRM Sync**: Push qualified leads to HubSpot/Salesforce

### Analytics

Comprehensive insights into agent performance:

- **Call Metrics**: Duration, outcome, sentiment analysis
- **Conversion Tracking**: Lead to opportunity pipeline
- **Quality Evaluations**: AI-assisted QA scoring
- **Performance Insights**: Trends and recommendations
